# coffeetealite.github.io Poem Spin-off of "Challenge: Write a Poem"

The design of words


spun out finely, inserted into pieces of
art, telling stories akin to experience
printed with care, some wrapped in lace
some cause tears to roll down a pretty face
--
though some are cruel
most are luxurious and kind
the design of them is peculiar
yet exquisite and unique
